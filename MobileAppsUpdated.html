Databases

AccessMyLibrary by Gale

Search Gale databases for how-to-guides, magazine, journal, and encyclopedia articles. Works within a 10-mile radius of your location.

Compatible with iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad, and Android devices.

Download the app.

EBSCOhost Mobile

Search EBSCO databases and read articles on your iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad, or Android device

Download the app.

Authenticate the app with Washington County Library:

  • Open EBSCO MegaFILE.
  • Click on the EBSCOhost iPhone and Android Applications link in the page footer.
  • Enter your email address and follow instructions in the authorization email.

 

ReferenceUSA

Research companies and executives, find addresses and phone numbers, and search for jobs, or new business opportunities.

Download iPad app.

To log on:

  • Enter your library barcode number.
  • Select MELSA for your library.

 

Byki Mobile Community Edition

Practice your Transparent Language Online lessons on your mobile device. Compatible with iPhone, iPod Touch, iPad, and Android devices.

Download the app.

Authenticate app with Washington County Library:

  • Create an user account on Transparent Language Online and log on.
  • Click Explore.
  • Click Go Mobile.
  • Click Get Byki CLE User Name and copy the code to your app to authenticate.
