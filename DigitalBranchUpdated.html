eMagazines on Zinio

 

WCL Digital Magazine Collection

Read popular magazines on your mobile device or computer with Zinio for Libraries, a digital magazine service with a new single login for your tablet or computer. Download issues and read at your leisure - no due dates! You just need a library card registered with Washington County Library.

Apps are available for:

  • iPhone and Android
  • iPad and Kindle Fire
  • MAC and PC, or use any web browser
